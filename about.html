Hi, I'm Mellissa He

I’m an industrial and UX designer passionate about crafting thoughtful, accessible, and intuitive design solutions. I combine physical and digital thinking to design for real-world impact.

My projects range from revitalizing the town of San Blas through installations grounded on ethnographic research, to playful sign language tools for kids, to ceramics inspired by pre-Incan archeological discoveries. I believe design should be thoughtful, tactile, and a little whimsical—because improving lives doesn’t have to be boring.
